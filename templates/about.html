About ElTrack

Our Story

ElTrack was founded in 2022 with a simple yet powerful mission: to protect elephants from the increasing dangers they face in modern landscapes, particularly along railway corridors where tragic accidents have occurred.

Our team of wildlife conservationists, engineers, and data scientists came together with a shared vision of using technology to create safer environments for wild elephants while promoting coexistence with human activities.

What started as a small pilot project in southern India has now grown into a sophisticated tracking and alert system that helps prevent elephant-train collisions and monitors elephant movements near electrical installations and other hazards.

Our Technology

ElTrack combines cutting-edge LoRa (Long Range) wireless technology with sophisticated tracking algorithms to monitor elephant movements in real-time.

LoRa Tracking System

Our LoRa-based tracking collars provide long-range communication with low power consumption, allowing for months of continuous tracking on a single battery charge.

Real-time Mapping

Advanced mapping and GIS technology enables real-time visualization of elephant locations relative to potential hazards such as railway tracks and electrical fences.

Alert System

Our traffic light warning system provides clear alerts when elephants are approaching hazardous areas, enabling rapid response from railway operators and wildlife authorities.
